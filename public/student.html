Enroll Student

Begin Your Journey with Us

Congratulations on your acceptance to ATALAY School! As an enrolled student, you are now part of a dynamic and diverse community committed to academic excellence, personal growth, and global impact. Our vibrant campus is eager to welcome you, offering countless opportunities to explore your interests, deepen your knowledge, and develop the skills you need for a successful future.

Academic Calendar and Course Registration

Stay informed about important dates and deadlines by regularly checking the academic calendar. This includes information on the start and end dates of semesters, registration periods, holidays, and exam schedules. Course registration is a crucial process that allows you to select and enroll in the classes you need for your program. Make sure to register for courses early to secure your preferred schedule and avoid any last-minute complications. If you need assistance with course selection, our academic advisors are here to help.

Student Services and Support

At ATALAY School, we are committed to providing you with the support you need to succeed. Our student services include academic advising, career counseling, mental health resources, and tutoring centers. If you encounter any academic or personal challenges, do not hesitate to reach out to these services. Additionally, our student health center offers medical care and wellness programs to ensure your physical and mental well-being throughout your time here.

Financial Aid and Scholarships

If you require financial assistance, explore the various financial aid options and scholarships available at ATALAY School. Our financial aid office can provide information and guidance on applying for grants, loans, and scholarships to help you manage the cost of your education. Make sure to meet all application deadlines and provide the necessary documentation to ensure timely processing of your financial aid requests.


By staying informed and taking advantage of the resources and opportunities available to you, you can make the most of your time at ATALAY School. We are excited to have you as part of our community and look forward to supporting you on your academic and personal journey.

Candidate Student

Discover Your Potential

At ATALAY School, we believe in the unique potential of every student. As a candidate student, you are about to embark on a transformative journey where you will discover your strengths, passions, and aspirations. Our dedicated faculty and staff are here to support you every step of the way, providing the guidance and resources you need to excel academically and personally.

Campus Resources and Facilities

Take full advantage of the numerous resources and facilities available on campus. Our libraries are equipped with extensive collections of books, journals, and digital resources to support your research and study needs. The campus also features modern laboratories, computer labs, and study spaces to enhance your learning experience. For those interested in fitness and recreation, our sports facilities include gyms, swimming pools, and various sports courts.

International Opportunities

Embark on an unforgettable adventure with Erasmus+ at ATALAY School! Study abroad at one of our esteemed European partner institutions, immerse yourself in a new culture, enhance your language skills, and gain invaluable international experience. The Erasmus+ program fosters academic and personal growth, helping you build lifelong friendships and professional networks. Join Erasmus+ and transform your university experience into a global exploration, preparing you for a dynamic, interconnected world.

Your Future Starts Here

As you consider your future, remember that ATALAY School is more than just a place to study; it is a place to grow, explore, and achieve your dreams. We are excited to welcome you into our community and to support you on your journey to becoming the best version of yourself. Your future starts here, and we can't wait to see what you will accomplish.


Welcome to ATALAY School, where your potential knows no bounds and your future is bright. We look forward to seeing you on campus and being a part of your extraordinary journey.
