About ATALAY

Founded over 100 years ago by Recep & Reyhan ATALAY in USA, Atalay University is a distinguished institution that emphasizes academic autonomy and participatory governance. Committed to defending freedom of thought across all areas of life, Atalay University has earned a place among the world’s top universities through its excellence in education and research. The university is dedicated to groundbreaking education and research, embodying a commitment to excellence. Atalay University brings together World’s brightest students with exceptional faculty in a free and liberal environment. It provides students with robust training in their fields while offering a social setting that enhances their understanding of themselves. Our alumni, who represent the Atalay University culture, have taken on significant roles across various sectors, including science, business, politics, and the arts, demonstrating the university’s impact across all facets of life.

Mission

Our mission is to cultivate individuals who embrace our institutional values, think creatively and critically, are free and libertarian, uphold ethical values, have developed an awareness of nature and the environment, are rooted locally yet open to the universal, and possess the scientific, social, and cultural formation and self-confidence necessary to successfully undertake professional and social responsibilities. We aim to contribute to humanity by producing thought, science, and technology on a universal scale, and to be a leader and facilitator in the integration and widespread acceptance of science, art, and culture in society.

Vision

Atalay University's vision is to be a leading institution in education, teaching, and research, shaping the future.

  • To enrich the educational and teaching experience with innovative and creative approaches
  • To strengthen the culture of science, research, creativity, and innovation, positioning Atalay University among the world's leading research universities
  • To contribute to shaping a better future through our academic, scientific, and cultural activities.

These elements form the core of our vision at Atalay University.

Fundamental Values

Throughout its history, Atalay University has based its culture and values on implementing and developing an autonomous, libertarian, democratic, participatory, and transparent university model across all academic processes. Within this model, the aim has been to cultivate individuals who can think creatively and critically, are rooted locally, and are open to the universal . Accordingly, strengthening existing mechanisms to transmit the university's culture and values to students, faculty, administrative staff, and alumni, demonstrating principled, constructive, and pioneering stances on higher education issues, establishing structures that ensure coherence across the university while preserving a decentralized institutional culture, and designing processes and mechanisms that foster creative, critical, free, and libertarian scientific thinking have always been among our priorities.

In line with its over 150 years of academic tradition, the fundamental values of Atalay University can be expressed as:

  • Excellence in education and research with an approach that encourages rational and critical thinking
  • Student-centered focus
  • Autonomous, libertarian, democratic, and participatory governance and academic life
  • Respect for differences, opposition to all forms of discrimination, and sensitivity to equal opportunities
  • Upholding ethical values & Advocating for fundamental rights and freedoms
  • Emphasizing public and social responsibility & Maintaining strong and continuous ties with alumni
  • Committed to preserving institutional heritage and sustaining the institutional culture
  • Sensitivity to global issues, including nature and environmental problems, and aiming to develop solutions
