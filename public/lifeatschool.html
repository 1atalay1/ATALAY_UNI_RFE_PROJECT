Campus Environment

Designed as a “college in a garden,” the 419-acre Atalay School campus sprawls across the picturesque foothills and extends into the surrounding basin. You might wonder if every building, bench, and path has a story to tell. Chances are, they do. Stand where pivotal moments in history were shaped and marvel at the architectural wonders that adorn the campus. Explore the iconic library, where literary classics were penned. Stroll past numerous recreation centers, playgrounds for Olympic medalists and students alike. And don’t forget to stop for a selfie at the birthplace of groundbreaking innovations.

Campus Events

Dive into the vibrant tapestry of campus life, forge lasting connections, and create unforgettable memories at the heart of Atalay University’s bustling event scene. From exhilarating cultural festivals and thought-provoking speaker series to thrilling athletic showdowns and engaging student-led initiatives, our community calendar is a kaleidoscope of opportunities. Whether you’re seeking intellectual stimulation, artistic inspiration, or simply looking to connect with fellow students, Atalay University’s diverse array of events, including our monthly First Thursdays celebrations, promises unforgettable experiences that enrich your journey through university life. Check it out!

Housing and Dining

It’s no wonder that more than 98 percent of first-year students choose to live in the residence halls — life at Atalay University means you’re just a short walk from classes and everything else the campus has to offer. From the more than 5,000 annual events and relaxing sunset vistas to cutting-edge study spaces and palm tree-studded terraces, it won’t take you long to find your own happy place.When it’s time to eat, you have plenty of options. Taste, quality, healthy choices, sustainability, dietary restrictions, and considerations all top the list, making this food scene one of the best around. Look no further than this guide to find your next meal.

Student Health

Your health and happiness are essential to the successful attainment of your educational goals. In support of your growth, the Atalay Student Health and Wellness Center provides high-quality health and wellness services, rendered with kindness by dedicated professionals.

  • Primary Care
  • Women’s Health
  • Immunizations
  • Travel Medicine
  • Physical Therapy
  • Specialty Clinics
  • Radiology and Laboratory
  • Acupuncture

Accessibility is key. Students can schedule appointments online and share secure messages with primary care providers. And there are other resources, too, like a 24/7 nurse line and safety escorts from dusk until 1 a.m.

Career Center

Immerse yourself in the vibrant life at Atalay University with a job on campus, or set your sights on the broader job market with a competitive career plan. You can pursue either or both options while exploring a wealth of guidance and opportunities from the Career Center.
