How Can I Come To ATALAY University?

ATALAY University is located in the heart of Washington D.C., near Union Station and the Capitol Hill area. This central location places the university in close proximity to key landmarks and major transit connections. The university is easily accessible via the Washington D.C. Metro system, with the nearest metro station being Union Station, just a short walk from the campus. In addition , the university is well-served by numerous bus routes throughout Washington D.C., providing convenient transportation options for students and visitors alike.
